What I’ve been up to

Totnes Convergence

Totnes Convergence is a participatory event in Totnes and Dartington, Devon, UK for 300 people, Thursday 4th - Sunday 7th June 2020 that I initiated and am curating alongside Hana Wolf, Phoebe Tickell, Tarn Rodgers Johns, Daniel Hernandez and Chloe Wykes.

Themes include: complexity, ecology & regeneration · decentralised organising · entheogens & consciousness · magic, witchcraft & indigeneity · metamodern politics, new economics & Game B · movement, touch & play · music & performance · the great transition

If you appreciate my work/this newsletter, you’re going to love Totnes Convergence. Read more and apply via Autopia
